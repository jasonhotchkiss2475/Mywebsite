About

I grew up in the world of action sports and as a young motocross racer, I always wanted to be the best I could be and consistently looking to edge out the competition through either fitness training or equipment upgrades. That sort of mindset continued through whatever hobby or career choice I made and continues to this very day.

Art has always been in my blood as well and when I wanted to perform the best I could, also had to have the coolest looking gear to show, from custom painted helmets to matching designs on the bike, but that's not all the art I was trying to show off! Graphic design was an interest of mine since a young age, but as an adult, the hands-on experience led me to build a classic Triumph motorcycle and a 1949 Ford hotrod. Metalwork and painting are so much fun!

There's much more to this story though as computer programs such as Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and now creating cool looking, responsive, functional websites have piqued my interest with all the critical thinking and problem solving to produce a one-off design. Although the design draws me in the most, having a flawless working website is just like having a well-oiled machine that I built!!!
